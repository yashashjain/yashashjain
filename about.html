Personal Statement

Consistency, Dedication, and a Positive attitude have been the three most crucial factors contributing to my success in my personal and professional endeavours so far. I was born in a town by the name Bagidora, Rajasthan.

I'm Yashash. I'm enthusiastic about new emerging computing techniques like in-memory computing, and probabilistic computing. Very keen to expand my knowledge envelope to contribute to the betterment of the world.

Education

I did my M.Tech in Microelectronics and VLSI Design from IISc Bangalore. I worked in SINESys Lab under the guidance of Prof. Utsav Banerjee on Designing compact, configurable and scalable hardware accelerator for probabilistic computing. I am very excited to expand my knowledge envelope.

Professional Experience and Education

Work is focused on data acquisition of wideband chirp radar signal at a very high sampling rate and design of digital sub-system for processing. Developing optimized, configurable and scalable hardware solutions to tackle multichannel real-time data processing.
