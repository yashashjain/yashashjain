005 : Smart Soldier Strap

Project Title:
Novel wearable device for health monitoring and tracking of soldiers based on LoRa module

Abstract:
This proposed embedded system is a wearable entity that can be mounted on soldier’s body to measure their body vital parameters (Blood pressure, temperature & oxygen levels)along with Current location of individual Soldiers.

Smart sensors are attached with the soldiers and other components like LCD, Batteries, sensors and PCB are enclosed in a box, which will be in the form of band.
